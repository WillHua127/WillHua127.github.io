About

Hi, I am a research master student at McGill & Mila working on AI for drug discovery and biology. I am fortunately supervised by Doina Precup and Guy Wolf. I received a bachelor degree in computer science (with first-class honours) at McGill & Mila, where I completed my thesis under supervision of William Hamilton on graph neural networks and heterophily. You can find my CV.

I am interested in machine learning with a focus on representation learning and generative models for biological data. I am working on proteins and enzymes recently.

I am looking for full-time PhD positions starting in Fall 2025! Please feel free to reach out to me for any information :)
